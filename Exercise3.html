How to Make Scrambled Eggs

  1. Whisk the eggs until you don't see streaks of yolk and whites.
  2. Heat the nonstick pan over medium to low heat.
  3. Gently pour the eggs into the pan.
  1. Scramble the eggs constantly so that they cook evenly.
  2. Add chopped herbs, or bits of meat or cheese if desired.
  3. Gently put the eggs onto a plate and you're done.

How to Get Rid of Hiccups

  • Breathe into a paper bag but don't put it over your head.
  • Pull your knees towards your chest and lean forward.
  • Sip ice-cold water.
  • Swallow some granulated sugar.
  • Bite on a lemon or taste vinegar.
  • Hold your breath for a short duration.

How to to Tie a Bow Tie

They should be different lengths.
- Start with one side longer than the other. There should be a two to three inches difference in length.
Make a simple knot.
- Cross both sides and tuck the longer end up and over.
Pinch a bow.
- Fold the shorter end into the shape of a bow.
Pull and pluck.
- Pull the long side over the bow and then both sides of the bow together.
Push it through the loop
- There should be a hole in the bow near your neck, a loop behind the bow portion. Fold the middle of the dangling end of the bow tie and push it through the loop.
Adjust
- Adjust the bow tie to your liking.

How to Not Lose in Tic Tac Toe

  • If you get the first move, take a corner.
    • If your opponent takes the center, it can end in a draw.
    • If your opponent takes another position other than the center, then you can win.
  • If you get the second move:
    • Do something that forces a tie.
    • Hope that your opponent makes a mistake.
